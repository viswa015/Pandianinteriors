Questions & Answers

01

Do you provide 360-degree 3D walkthroughs?

Yes, every project includes a high-definition 360° virtual tour during the design phase so you can experience your home before we begin production.
02

How long does a typical project take?

A full residential redesign usually takes 4 to 12 months, depending on structural changes and lead times for PANDIAN furniture.
03

What is included in the "Free Estimate"?

Our free estimate includes a site visit (if local), a mood board concept, and a detailed line-item budget breakdown for materials and labor.
